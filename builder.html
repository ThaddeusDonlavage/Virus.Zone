Character Builder

A quick step-by-step creation flow for players to build COMP users.

1 — Concept

Choose a concept (Net-Runner, Street Doctor, Enforcer, Fixer, Ritualist).

2 — Attributes

Distribute 10 points across Strength, Reflex, Will, Intellect, Empathy (minimum 1 each). Example: Strength 2, Reflex 3, Intellect 3, Will 1, Empathy 1.

3 — Skills

Choose 3 starting skills. Skills grant +X to rolls where applicable.

4 — COMP Selection

Pick a COMP model: Basic (utility), Adaptive (learning), or Ritual (esoteric effects). COMPs add abilities and may require Corruption checks when using powerful features.

5 — Gear & Starting Stats

Select starter gear and record Stamina and Sanity pools. Default Stamina: 10 + Strength. Sanity: 6 + Will.

Example Build

Name: Sel
Concept: Net-Runner
Attributes: Strength 2, Reflex 3, Intellect 4, Will 1, Empathy 1
COMP: Adaptive
Skills: Hacking, Electronics, Stealth
Gear: Pulse Deck, Crowbar, Medkit

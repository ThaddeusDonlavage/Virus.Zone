Influencer

We never know which lives we influence, or when, or why.

— Steven King

Socialite / Performer / Actor

Influencers never fight alone. They fight through networks, belief systems, and attention. Their power grows the more eyes are on their persona, the more voices repeat them, the more reality itself bends to their will. In combat, they empower allies, destabilize enemies, and turn momentum into a weapon that feeds itself.

Sigma

Do not pray for an easy life, pray for the strength to endure a difficult one.

— Bruce Lee

Skirmisher / Striker / Survivor

A Sigma thrives on a belief that they are completely independent. In battle, they assess threats, outmaneuver chaos, and punish through sheer willpower and marksmanship. Whether wielding a weapon or relying on just their physical prowess, they believe victory belongs to the prepared, not the protected.

Character Advancement

On a level up, you may add one bonus to your Operating System by rolling on the table. Abilities added this way are considered to be part of the Core Operating System and do not count towards your Chip total.

1
+1 to one Mind Stat
2
+1 to one Body Stat
3
+1 to one Spirit Stat
4
Add one "COMBO" tag Chip to your COMP.
5
Add the "Source Run" Chip to your COMP.
6
+1 Sturdy Added to your COMP.
7
+1 to the Maximum number of weapon modifiers.
8
Add the "Hard Point" Chip to your COMP.
9
+1 to your Base Defense
10
Add the "Quick Scope" Chip to your COMP.

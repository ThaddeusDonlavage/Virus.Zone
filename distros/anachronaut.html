Engineer / Technician / Gadgeteer

Anachronauts, for better or worse have become deeply embedded within The Veil. As a consequence not experience time as a straight line. To them, moments overlap, echo, and occasionally argue with one another. They are deeply attuned with technology as it exists and have a great ability to modify and twist the mundane in ways that they shouldn't. Mentally scarred from the circuitry with no way to heal, Anachronauts often find themselves delving deeper and deeper into Cyberspace for an attempt to find a home.

Universe Lore

The Veil is thinning — and the Virus spreads.

VIRUS.ZONE

The Veil: A boundary-layer for any unexplained phenomena where reality and algorithmic processes blur. As the Veil thins, a physical area begins to slip into "mixed reality states". Most observers note observe mythical or religious properties within a digital framework, all manifesting in tangible form.

cosmOS: The recursive, semi-sentient meta-network underlying all computation. A fully distributed Operating System, its layers include artifical entities, and a feedback loop of signals which provide a simulacra of a physical space by which some may access.

COMP: A spiritual resonant computing device, capable of interfacing cosmOS and identifying instances of The Veil. Part spirit-familiar, part front-end navigation device with a main operation of evoking psychic phenomena

Core Mechanics

Quick reference for resolution, dice, and outcomes.

Basic Resolution

Roll 1d20 + relevant Attribute + Skill. Compare to a target Difficulty (DC).

Outcomes

  • Success: Meets or beats DC — action succeeds.
  • Critical Success: Natural 20 — enhanced effect + GM bonus.
  • Failure: Falls short of DC — partial or no effect.
  • Critical Failure: Natural 1 — complicating outcome.

Combat Flow (Turn Order)

  1. Initiative roll (1d20 + Reflex)
  2. Actions: Move, Act, Use COMP, Reaction
  3. Resolve damage/effects and status updates

Damage & Armor

Damage reduces Stamina. Armor reduces incoming damage by its Rating before Stamina loss.

Sample Roll

Roll: 1d20 + Strength(3) + Skill(2) => Result 18 vs DC15 => Success

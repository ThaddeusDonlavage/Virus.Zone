Character Creation

Steps to build a COMP user or other denizen of the Virtual Veil.

Step 1 — Concept & Background

Choose your character's origin: Corporate, Underground, AI-syndicate, or Fringe.

Step 2 — Attributes

Main attributes: Strength, Reflex, Will, Intellect, Empathy. Roll or assign points.

Step 3 — Skills & Talents

Pick core skills (hacking, negotiation, firearms, engineering). Talents provide niche bonuses.

Step 4 — COMP Contract

Select a COMP: Basic, Adaptive, or Ritual. COMPs grant abilities and can decay under Virus influence.

Step 5 — Starting Gear & HP

Choose starter gear. Determine Stamina and Sanity (or equivalent) pools.

Example

Name: Dara-7
Class: Net-Runner
Attributes: Intellect 4, Reflex 3, Will 2
COMP: Adaptive (basic remote override)

Notes

Character sheets are intended to be compact; this panel provides the essentials for quick creation at the table.

COMP

How COMPs interact in conflict and the basics of resolving encounters.

COMP Basics

COMPs are bonded devices that supply programs, reactions, and defensive measures. They have Integrity (HP), Energy, and Corruption levels.

Combat Flow

  1. Initiative roll (d20 + Mind).
  2. Each turn: Major action + Minor action.
  3. COMPs can spend energy to grant extra minor actions or program effects.

Example Encounter (Short)

A Runner hacks a turret: d20 + Mind + Hacking vs DR. On success, turret disabled. On failure, turret fires and may corrupt the COMP.

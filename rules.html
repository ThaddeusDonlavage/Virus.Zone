Game Rules

Core rules and tableflow for Virtual Veil, summarized for quick reference.

Core Concept

Actions are resolved with a single roll: 1d20 + Attribute + Skill vs Difficulty (DC). Use the Core Mechanics panel for full resolution examples.

Character Progression

Characters advance by acquiring Experience (XP) through missions. XP buys attribute points, skills, or COMP upgrades. See Character Builder for creation rules.

Sanity & Corruption

The Virus introduces Corruption points. When a character accumulates Corruption they gain unstable abilities and risk permanent changes. Corruption can be resisted via Will checks.

Table Rules

  • Keep rulings fast — default to simple outcomes when unsure.
  • Use Panels for quick lookup; expand rules in full rulebook drafts later.
  • Players and GM should agree on ambiguous consequences ahead of time.
